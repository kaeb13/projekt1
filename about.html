ABOUT US

Alexander Hamilton Fan Club

We're a community of passionate Alexander Hamilton fans who come together to celebrate the life and legacy of one of America's most fascinating founding fathers. Our club has been active for over 5 years and has grown to over 500 members. We welcome anyone who shares our love for Hamilton and his contributions to American history, from casual fans to history buffs and beyond.

Our club meets regularly at various locations around the city, from coffee shops to museums and historical sites. We discuss all things Hamilton, from the hit musical to his political career and personal life. Our events include book clubs, museum tours, trivia nights, and even special performances of Hamilton-related shows. We also organize trips to other cities and states for Hamilton-related events and historical sites.

In addition to our in-person events, our online community is active and engaging. We have a private forum where members can share news, updates, and their own research about Hamilton and his era. We also have a Facebook group where members can connect and share their love for all things Hamilton.

Membership in our club is open to anyone who shares our passion for Alexander Hamilton and his contributions to American history. We encourage you to join us at our next event or meeting, connect with us online, and become part of our vibrant community. Let's keep the legacy of Alexander Hamilton alive and thriving!
